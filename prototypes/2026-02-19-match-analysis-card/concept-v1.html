About

We excel at early stage investments in ambitious founders who are building unusually great companies. Twenty Two has the conviction to invest earlier than most other investors and we're most excited by talking to companies and founders before they've publicly launched. Our check sizes tend to run smaller, allowing us to make decisions quickly and execute efficiently, something that differentiates us from larger funds and endears us to founders that just want to get back to work. We seek magnetic, tenacious, adaptable founders who are not afraid of big ideas and ultimately ship great things. If this sounds like you, we would love to talk.

Why This Match Works

TwentyTwo VC is a strong match for your seed-stage SaaS startup. Their focus on early-stage founders who haven't publicly launched aligns well with your current stage, and their sector coverage in SaaS, Fintech, and AI directly overlaps with your positioning.

Key Alignment Points

  • Stage: They invest at seed, often pre-launch. Your current stage is exactly their sweet spot.
  • Check size: $50K-$250K aligns with your target raise. They could lead or co-lead a round in this range.
  • Thesis: They value "magnetic, tenacious, adaptable founders" who ship. If you can demonstrate velocity, that resonates.
  • Decision speed: They explicitly call out fast decision-making as a differentiator.

Potential Concerns

  • Smaller check size means they may not fill your entire round alone

What should I emphasize when reaching out?

Lead with your shipping velocity and traction metrics. TwentyTwo values founders who "just want to get back to work" rather than pitch endlessly. A concise, specific outreach that shows what you've built and why you're reaching out to them specifically will land better than a generic deck. Mention a portfolio company of theirs in an adjacent space to show you've done your homework.
