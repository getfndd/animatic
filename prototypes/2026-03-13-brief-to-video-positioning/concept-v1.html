Design Principles

Constraints that create.

Personalities aren't limitations. They're features. Every constraint makes the output more intentional.

🎯

States, not keyframes

Define what your scenes are, not how they animate frame-by-frame. The engine handles timing, easing, and transitions based on personality rules.

🛡️

Guardrails, not slop

Every output is validated against camera physics, personality boundaries, and shot grammar. If it breaks a rule, it doesn't ship.

🔎

Traceable decisions

See exactly why each camera move, transition, and duration was chosen. Intent → personality → primitive → timing → render.

🎧

Beat-synced editing

Drop in audio. The planner detects beats, snaps transitions to the grid, and matches camera intensity to energy. Music video feel, automatically.

🔀

A/B choreography

Generate multiple style variants from the same scenes. Score them. Compare pacing, variety, flow, and adherence. Ship the best one.

🧰

LLM-enhanced, rule-safe

Claude improves scene text and suggests camera moves. But every LLM output passes through validateScene(). On failure, rule-based fallback.

Aesthetic Range

Four personalities. Infinite styles.

Each personality defines timing tiers, easing curves, camera rules, and forbidden features. Style packs layer on top. Custom personalities extend the set.
