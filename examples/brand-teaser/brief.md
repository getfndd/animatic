# Canvas — Brand Teaser Brief

## Product

Canvas is a design tool built for creative professionals who demand precision and freedom in equal measure.

## Audience

Designers, creative directors, and creative professionals working in branding, product design, and visual storytelling. They value tools that stay out of the way and let ideas flow.

## Promise

Design freedom without compromise. Canvas removes the friction between thought and execution, so every idea gets the space it deserves.

## Features

- **Real-time collaboration** — Multiple designers working on the same canvas simultaneously, with presence indicators and conflict-free editing.
- **Infinite canvas** — No artboard limits. Pan, zoom, and organize freely across an unbounded workspace.
- **Smart constraints** — Intelligent layout rules that adapt as you design, keeping alignment and spacing consistent without manual effort.

## Proof

Used by 50,000+ design teams worldwide, from independent studios to Fortune 500 creative departments.

## Tone

Cinematic-dark. Prestige. Confident and unhurried. Let the product speak through atmosphere, not feature lists.

## Format

20-second brand teaser. 5 scenes. 1920x1080 @ 60fps.

## Archetype

brand-teaser — atmosphere-led, minimal copy, product glimpse as emotional hook.
