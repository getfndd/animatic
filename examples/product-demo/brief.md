# Taskflow — Product Demo Brief

## Product

Taskflow is a project management tool that brings clarity to team coordination through visual workflows, timeline views, and real-time collaboration.

## Audience

Product managers, team leads, and operations professionals who juggle multiple projects and need a single source of truth for what's happening, what's blocked, and what's next.

## Promise

Clarity from chaos. Taskflow transforms scattered tasks, threads, and spreadsheets into a unified view that makes prioritization obvious and progress visible.

## Features

- **Drag-drop task boards** — Kanban-style boards with custom columns. Drag tasks between stages, assign owners, set due dates. Everything updates in real time.
- **Gantt timelines** — Visual timeline view showing task dependencies, milestones, and critical path. Zoom from day to quarter level.
- **Real-time sync** — Changes propagate instantly across all connected clients. No refresh, no conflicts, no stale data.

## Proof

10,000+ teams actively using Taskflow. 4.8-star average rating across app stores. Trusted by startups and enterprise teams alike.

## Tone

Neutral-light. Minimal. Clear and functional. Let the product UI demonstrate value through real interactions, not marketing language.

## Format

30-second product demo. 6 scenes. 1920x1080 @ 60fps.

## Archetype

feature-reveal — interaction-led, real UI demonstrations, proof through data.
