# Atlas AI — Prompt-to-Result Demo

A 25-second AI product demo showcasing Atlas AI, a research assistant that accelerates literature review.

## Audience

Researchers, academics, and knowledge workers who spend hours synthesizing academic papers. They value precision, citations, and depth over flashy interfaces.

## Promise

10x faster literature review — ask a natural language question, get structured summaries with citations in seconds.

## Features

- **Natural language queries** — Ask research questions in plain English
- **Citation extraction** — Automatic source attribution from every paper
- **Paper summarization** — Key findings distilled into structured summaries
- **Follow-up questions** — Conversational refinement of research queries

## Proof

- Used at 200+ universities worldwide
- 50M+ papers indexed
- Average query-to-result: 4.2 seconds

## Format

- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (16:9)
- **Duration:** 25 seconds (with crossfade overlaps)
- **Personality:** cinematic-dark
- **Style:** dramatic
- **Archetype:** ai-demo (5 scenes)
- **Transitions:** crossfade primary, focus_dissolve before result reveal
