# Freshly — Social Square Loop

A 12-second social media loop in 1:1 format for Freshly, a food delivery app.

## Audience

Urban professionals aged 25-40 who value convenience, health, and speed. They browse social feeds during commutes and lunch breaks — attention span under 3 seconds.

## Promise

Fast, healthy food delivery from local restaurants — dinner on the table in 15 minutes with zero delivery fees.

## Features

- **15-minute delivery** — Order to doorstep in under 15 minutes
- **$0 delivery fee** — Free delivery on every order, no minimum
- **Restaurant variety** — 500+ local restaurants, curated for quality

## Proof

- 4.9-star App Store rating (12K+ reviews)
- 2M+ deliveries completed
- Average delivery time: 13.2 minutes

## Format

- **Resolution:** 1080x1080 (square)
- **Duration:** 12 seconds
- **Personality:** montage
- **Style:** energy
- **Archetype:** social-loop (4 scenes, loop=true)
- **Transitions:** whip_left, hard_cut — no crossfades
